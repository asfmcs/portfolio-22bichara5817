Cancun Travel Guide

By Diego Bichara

Intro to Cancun

Cancún is a Mexican city located in the Yucatan Peninsula, in the state of Quintana Roo. It is a beautiful city full of beaches, ruins, and amazing relics. It also has lots of places to shop for amazing things like seashells.

Things to do in Cancun

In Cancún, Quintana Roo, México, there are lots of things to do. These include

  • White, sandy Zona Hotelera beaches
  • Travel to Tulum or Chichen Itza and see the ruins
  • Go to Cozumel or Playa del Cármen and relax on the beach
  • Go to Isla Mujeres on a ferry
  • Xcaret Theme Park

Zona Hotelera History

Cancun is a city with lots of history. For starters, the Zona Hotelera is relatively new, as it is man made! If you zoom in on a map, you can see a gap between the mainland and the Zona Hotelera. Age Friendly

Overall, Cancún is a pretty good destination for travels for people of all ages. Little children can play at the beach or go to the various theme parks, and adults can relax with a book, go shopping in the diverse malls, and see the Mayan ruins. Whenever you decide to plan a trip to the beach, consider Cancún for its amazing, beautiful beaches, diverse shopping locations, and rich history.
